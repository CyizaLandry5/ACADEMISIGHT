AcademiSight

AcademiSight is a modern, lightweight module-tracking platform designed to help universities manage students with incomplete, transferred, exempted, or previously covered modules.

It solves a major challenge: students losing modules or the HOD struggling to plan a correct timetable, especially with transferred, weekend, evening, or irregular students.

Who benefits?

  • Students: Never lose track of completed or pending modules.
  • Class Representatives: Easily request uncovered modules.
  • HOD / Coordinator: Effortless planning of timetables.
  • Universities: Smooth management of transferred and mixed-program students.

Why AcademiSight?

• Simple, fast, and built for real academic workflow.
• API-powered (Google Sheets + Apps Script).
• Clean module summaries for data-driven decisions.
• Built as a full-stack portfolio project — open source and expanding.
